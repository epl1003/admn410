Financing

  1. Use the DHP pet-card to earn rewards on how much you spend

Delivery

If you are within the town of Durham, we will deliver anything you may need, with no extra delivery fee charge. Anything outside the town of Durham will have an added on delivery fee, depending on the distance of the delivery.

Return Policies

We have a two week return policy on all toy items that have not recieved significant damage. If you return the item, then you will get 100% of the money back that you spent on the item. Things like pet food or treats will be unable to return once they have been opened.
